TaskPaper.com

Simple TaskPaper to-do list syncing

For TaskPaper users who want to sync their to-do lists online. TaskPaper.com stores your lists and syncs them between your work, home, and iPhone (coming soon). Unlike generic sync solutions TaskPaper.com integrates tightly with TaskPaper for the Mac.

  • Beta. Things may change and go wrong.
  • Human Sized. For your to-do list and notes. Not for huge files.
  • Light Security. Not for sensitive information, credit cards, passwords, etc.
